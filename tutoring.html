Tutoring and Teaching Experience

As a tutor and forever learner, I grasp the realities and workings of this world and strive to make this knowledge extremely accessible to others.

In the past, I've tutored voluntarily for my high school's National Honors Society (N.H.S.) - I tutored in Algebra II, Precalculus, Calculus I, and English. Through my proficient math and reading/writing comprehension skills, as well as my interpersonal and leadership skills, I helped struggling students raise their grades significantly. During my time at N.H.S., I tutored for 20 hours in total.

Currently, I work at Kumon Learning Center, where I help kids develop math (up to Calculus I) and literacy (up to high school level English) skills. I analyze kids' progress by timing their completion of worksheets and grading their accuracy. Using these analyses, I teach according to their skill level, heavily enhancing their academic prowess and enabling them to be ahead of their grade. I have been working here since August of 2018 (for approximately 1.5 years).

I am also a self-employed math tutor who bases their business through Wyzant.com. I tutor in Algebra 1, Algebra 2, and Precalculus on there. Click here to visit my Wyzant profile.
